Easy Coleslaw Dressing

Description

Easy Coleslaw Dressing is a simple and tangy dressing perfect for enhancing the flavor of crunchy cabbage and other vegetables in coleslaw. With just a few basic ingredients like mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, and seasonings, this dressing effortlessly brings a delightful balance of creamy richness and zesty acidity to your coleslaw, making it a versatile accompaniment to any meal.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
  3. Store in an airtight container.
