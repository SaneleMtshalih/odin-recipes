Awesome Pasta Salad

Description

The Awesome Pasta Salad is a refreshing medley of flavors and textures, combining al dente pasta with a colorful assortment of fresh vegetables, savory meats, and tangy dressing. Bursting with vibrant ingredients like crisp bell peppers, juicy tomatoes, and crunchy cucumbers, this salad offers a delightful contrast of tastes and a satisfying crunch in every bite. Tossed in a flavorful dressing featuring herbs, spices, and a hint of acidity, this pasta salad is a crowd-pleasing dish perfect for picnics, potlucks, or any occasion that calls for a deliciously satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fusilli (spiral) pasta
  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ pound provolone cheese, cubed
  • ½ pound salami, cubed
  • ¼ pound sliced pepperoni, cut in half
  • 1 large green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 (10 ounce) can black olives, drained
  • 1 (4 ounce) jar pimentos, drained
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle Italian salad dressing

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook fusilli pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain.
  3. Combine pasta with tomatoes, cheese, salami, pepperoni, green pepper, olives, and pimentos in a large bowl. Pour in salad dressing; toss to coat.
  4. Enjoy!
